Absolute leader
in the market

TECSIS has more than 40,000 blades in operation worldwide, and is the absolute leader in the U.S. with 50% market share, it is among the 70th Brazilian companies that most export their products, and is the 21st largest exporter in the State of São Paulo, according to the 2010 Yearbook of the Revista Comércio Exterior (Foreign Trade magazine). The Company also holds about 15% of the worldwide market share of blades for wind power generation, excluding China.

Brazilian technology

100% Brazilian, TECSIS is a global company, considered the world’s largest manufacturer of custom blades for wind power turbines. Founded in 1995, the Company is an icon of innovation and technology and is among the world’s most innovative companies in the clean energy generation sector, providing top engineering solutions to major players in the business.

Production

TECSIS is the only one in the wind turbine blade industry able to meet customer demands throughout the whole value chain, TECSIS mix technology and pro-activity to manufacture its goods, molds and accessories. It makes prototypes and carries out tests, producing on a large-scale, combining land and sea transportation via an integrated logistics, approach and provides after-sales services.

Reference

Quoted in important international economic publications such as The Economist and Harvard Business Review, the Brazilian TECSIS company is a worldwide benchmark in its area of expertise and takes part of the international agenda foruns of discussions on wind energy. Recently, the company was praised by U.S. Secretary of State, Hillary Clinton, as an example of successful trading relationship between Brazil and U.S.

Awards

The company won the Foreign Trade Distinction Award twice; in 2009, in the Capital Goods category, and in 2006 in the Technological Highlight category.

The award honors a select group of exporting firms and is awarded annually by the Foreign Trade Association (AEB) and the Ministry of Development, Industry and Foreign Trade (MDIC).

Employment
Generation

Nowadays TECSIS employs more than 7,000 employees in its 12 manufacturing plants located in Sorocaba (SP) and Itu (SP).
